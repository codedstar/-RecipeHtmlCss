Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente, about 8-10 minutes.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the skillet with the garlic. Toss to coat the spaghetti with the garlic and olive oil.
  4. Remove the skillet from heat and add the Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
